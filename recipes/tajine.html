Tajine

Description

To make a Moroccan tagine, you can use the following simple recipe featuring chicken, spices, and vegetables:

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced thinly
  • 4-6 chicken thighs, skin-on and bone-in
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup olives, pitted
  • 1 preserved lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 cups chicken stock (or water)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: dried fruits like apricots or raisins, fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a tagine or heavy pot over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion, saut√©ing until softened.
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper, and add them to the pot, browning on all sides.
  3. Sprinkle the spices over the chicken, stirring to combine.
  4. Add the olives, preserved lemon, and enough chicken stock to partially cover the chicken. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Stir occasionally and add more liquid if necessary.
  6. If using, add dried fruits in the last 10 minutes of cooking. Garnish with parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Serve the tagine with crusty bread to soak up the flavorful sauce.
