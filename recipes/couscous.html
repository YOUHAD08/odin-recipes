Moroccan Couscous

Description

This Moroccan couscous recipe is a delightful combination of fluffy grains, spices, and vibrant vegetables, perfect as a side dish or a main meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 1/4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, cooked or canned
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, bring the vegetable broth (or water) to a boil. Add the couscous, remove from heat, and cover. Let it sit for 5 minutes to absorb the liquid.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced bell pepper, zucchini, and carrot. Sauté until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Fluff the couscous with a fork and add it to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Mix in the chickpeas and spices, stirring to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Serve the couscous warm, alongside grilled meats or as a vegetarian main dish.
